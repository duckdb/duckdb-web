    EendDB ("eend" is Dutch for "duck") is the official Dutch-language SQL dialect for DuckDB.
    After years of demand from the Dutch-speaking data engineering community, SQL queries can
    now be written in fluent Nederlands.


    The extension provides a complete localization of DuckDB's SQL syntax, including
    SELECTEER (SELECT), VAN (FROM), WAARBIJ (WHERE), GROEP PER (GROUP BY),
    VOLGORDE PER (ORDER BY), SAMENVOEGEN (JOIN), TOEVOEGEN AAN (INSERT INTO),
    BIJWERKEN (UPDATE), VERWIJDEREN VAN (DELETE FROM), MAAK TABEL (CREATE TABLE),
    and GOOI_WEG TABEL (DROP TABLE), among others.
