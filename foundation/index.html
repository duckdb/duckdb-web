DuckDB Foundation

Our Purpose

The independent non-profit DuckDB Foundation safeguards the long-term maintenance and development of DuckDB. The foundation holds most of the intellectual property (IP) of the project. The DuckDB Foundation is funded by charitable donations. All collected funds go directly to DuckDB development. A number of organizations have thankfully chosen to support the DuckDB project.
