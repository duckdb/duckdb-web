DuckDB can be compiled on the first MacBook Pro Retina and run TPC-H scale factor 1000.
This hardware is more than 10 years old and is from an era where the consensus was that “Big Data” needs distributed processing.
This begs the question: did we really [lose a decade of “Small Data”]({% post_url 2025-05-19-the-lost-decade-of-small-data %})?
