The 2021 MacBook Pro M1 Max with 64 GB RAM can complete all queries of the [TPC-DS workload]({% link docs/stable/core_extensions/tpcds.md %}) on the SF1,000 dataset (which uses 1 TiB of space when stored in CSV format).
It does not require any further configuration (default number of thread, memory, etc.) and achieves a geometric mean runtime of 7.5 seconds.
